## ⚠️ Methodology and caveats

This selection focuses on domains connected to technology terms rather than a single extension or naming style. Examples such as data.ryukyu, coding.ryukyu, virtual.new, and virtual.hamburg show a mix of direct keyword names across many TLDs. When comparing these domains, start with commercial clarity: does the keyword describe a real product, category, or use case? Then weigh the extension. A strong keyword can help memorability, but TLD choice affects trust, resale depth, and buyer confidence. With a median ask of $1,244.80 across 20,518 domains, pricing discipline matters most when narrowing this set.

- Keywords center on technology terms like data, coding, and virtual
- The selection spans 505 TLDs, so extension quality varies
- Median ask is $1,244.80 for this set of domains
- Favor names with clear use, easy recall, and lower confusion risk
